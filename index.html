About Me

I'm Ghxst — a security enthusiast operating across both sides of the battlefield. On defense, I build tools like XEngine, an explanation and reasoning engine that analyzes security events, interprets alerts, and helps teams cut through noise during incident response. On offense, I develop GhxstRecon AI — a lightweight, AI-assisted penetration testing helper that analyzes inputs, runs rule-based checks, and optionally leverages an LLM to surface actionable recon insights. Bridging the gap between red and blue through purple teaming and adversary emulation. Driven by penetration testing, threat simulation, and the unknown. Explore more of my work below.
